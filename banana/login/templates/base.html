Student Managemet

The Goverments are making efforts towards improving access to education for all children. Aside from making this possible there is also a need to make sure that the children remain in school and are properly educated. Therefore this system makes information readily available to be used for:

    • Evaluating the performance of each educational institution
      Evaluating the Development of each student
      Strategic Planning
      Resource Allocation
      Monitoring and Evaluating Performance

    • Asset Management

    Management of all the infrastructural item (Classroom Building, Vehicles, Computer Equipments, Lavatories, etc), which are provide for the monitoring and wellbeing of the institution.

    Institutional Management

    The Educatinal Institution covered by the Gorvenment (Primary Schools, Junior Secondry Schools, and Senior secondry Schools)

    Teacher Management

    Teachers' capacity development is expected to be given proper attention in order to ensure hat pupils and students are properly educated. This module covers (Postings, Academic development, Conpitenct identification and enhancement, Skills Sets Development, In house traning, Appraisal, Release.

    Project Management

    The module manges projects from the World Bank and other bodies for the enhancement of Education. Such funds and intervention are properly documented and segemnted with analysis here
